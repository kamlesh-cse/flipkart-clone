Shipping Policy

Delivery Estimates

Standard delivery usually takes 3 to 7 business days, depending on your location. We strive to process orders within 24 hours of placement.

Shipping Charges

Shipping is free for all orders above ₹499. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of ₹40 will be applied.
