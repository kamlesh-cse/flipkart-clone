Cancellation & Returns

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order any time before it has been shipped. Once shipped, the order cannot be cancelled, but you can refuse delivery or initiate a return after receipt.

Return Policy (7-Day Return)

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. The items must be returned in their original packaging with all tags and accessories included.

Refund Process

Once the return is received and inspected, we will initiate a refund to your original payment method. For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, we request bank details for the transfer.
